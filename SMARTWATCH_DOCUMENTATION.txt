        endpoints, battery ADC readings, and kernel routines.

   - Cellular Modem & SIM Integration:
     * AT+CPIN? -> READY (Inserted SIM Card Detected)
     * AT+CREG? -> Registered on Home Cellular Network
     * AT+CGATT=1 -> GPRS Attached
     * AT+CSTT="internet" -> PDP Context Activated (IP: 10.142.68.19)
